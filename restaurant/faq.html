Frequently Asked Questions

Is your meat Halal?

Yes, 100%. We use only HMC certified Halal meat for all our dishes.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes! We deliver within a 5-mile radius. You can order directly through our website or call us.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely. We have a wide range of vegetarian dishes including Daal Makhani, Palak Paneer, and Vegetable Biryani.

Can I book a table for a large party?

Yes, we can accommodate large groups. Please use the "Book Table" form on our Contact page or call us in advance.

Do you do catering?

Yes, we provide catering for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. Contact us for a custom quote.
