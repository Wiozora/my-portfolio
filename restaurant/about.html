Our Story

A Taste of Heritage

Founded in 2010, Zaiqa started with a simple mission: to bring the authentic, unadulterated flavors of Lahore's food streets to your plate. "Zaiqa" means "flavor" in Urdu, and that is exactly what we promise.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations. We believe in slow cooking, fresh ingredients, and the magic of hand-ground spices. From our clay oven (Tandoor) to our Karahi woks, every method is traditional.

We are not just a restaurant; we are a celebration of Pakistani culture and hospitality. When you dine with us, you are family.
